BroForms: The Free and Open-Source Alternative to Formspree

Collect form data without sacrificing your users' privacy or your wallet

  Get startd with this example 
  HTML
  
    <form 
        action="https://broforms.com/forms/{ form_id }"
        method="POST"
    >

      <input 
          type="text"
          name="name" 
          placeholder="Enter your name"
        >
      <input 
          type="email" 
          name="email" 
          placeholder="Enter your email"
      >
      <button 
          type="submit"
      >
        Submit
      </button>

    </form>

Note: This Data will be redirected to {form_id} at BroForms

BroForms is an open source forms application. It is developed by BroCode Tech Community and is open sourced on GitHub. You can find more information about it here.
